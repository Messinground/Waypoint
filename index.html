About

I just finished watching a video by Struthless and in it he mentioned something called a "digital garden." A place to post and catalogue content that enriches your sense of curiosity.

So a bit like social media or a blog, but the difference is that this is stuff that's formatted topically - not chronologically. Why should the most recent stuff be seen first? It's much more fun to naturally explore content as it relates to what you're already looking at.

I always thought waypoints felt like a kind of whimsical way to keep track of exploration. So welcome to my "Waypoint Atlas!"
