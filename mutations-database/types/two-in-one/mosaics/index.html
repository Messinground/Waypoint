Mosaics

Mosaics are those who posseses more than one genotype as a result of mutations in the early developmental stages of life.

The results are functionally similar to Chimerism, but the mechanism by which it comes about is different.

Mosaicism is most often made identifiable when it results in an organism possesing two different colors that are normally mutually exclusive.
