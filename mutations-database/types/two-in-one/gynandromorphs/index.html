Gynandromorphs

For species with sexual dimorphism (whose males and females have distinct characteristics unrelated to reproduction) mutations that are chimeric or mosaic in nature can often create what is called a Gynandromorph or Gynander.

Gynandromorphism the condition of having characteristics that are normally mutually exclusive to both male and females of the species.
